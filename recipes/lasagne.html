Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a layered pasta dish originating in Italy.

Layers of a beef mince sauce are alternated with layers of flat sheet pasta, topped with bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

Beef sauce/filling

  • 500g beef mince
  • 500g pork mince
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup beef stock
  • 30g tomato paste
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 stick celery
  • 500 cloves of garlic
  • 1 generous splash of red wine
  • 500g mushrooms
  • Family box of pasta sheets
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Bechamel sauce

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Nutmeg
