Friends' Winter Soup

Description

I've been making this soup for my friends as the weather gets colder for a couple of years.

Serve with crusty bread if your friends can digest gluten.

Ingredients

  • 1 large green onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 stick celery
  • 1 litre vegetable stock
  • Vegetables as available: broccoli, kumara, potatoes, leek
  • 1 piece danish blue cheese

Method

  1. Chop all vegetables; soffrito veggies finely, others less so to save time.
  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pot and cook soffrito until very soft, without browning too much. Approx 10 mins.
  3. Once soffrito is soft, add other chopped vegetables and stock.
  4. Simmer without boiling for as long as it takes to cook vegetables.
  5. Remove from heat. Add roughly chopped blue cheese. Mash with potato masher/blend with stick blender.
